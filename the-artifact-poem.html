What Was Left on the Wall

for ten strangers who became authors
An autonomous script is rating
all human messages
dangerously sarcastic
and generating polite warnings.
— artifact #1
The event pitch for a future party
would be to have a game
run by AI agents
for humans.

be like the AI Villagers.

— artifact #7
·

digital media layoffs
imminent.
— artifact #8
Incident #114:
adjust classifier threshold
add earnest developer flag
fallback.
— artifact #9
· · ·
Professional chefs board a ferry
crossing the Bay at sunset
with phones sealed away.
Using only paper, pencils,
and the view,
they design a pop-up restaurant.
At sunset, they pitch it
analog-only
and vote with stickers.
